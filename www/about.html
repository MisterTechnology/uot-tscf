Unitech Tertiary Students Christian Fellowship (TSCF) is a vibrant, Christ-centered student ministry based at the Papua New Guinea University of Technology (Unitech). We exist to glorify God by equipping and encouraging students to grow in faith, live out the gospel, and impact their campus, communities, and the nation for Christ..

Students Reaching Out to Students in TSCF Campus Ministry

At Unitech TSCF, we are committed to fostering an environment where students can thrive not only academically but also spiritually. Our dedication lies in ensuring that the university remains a place where young individuals grow in faith, purpose, and character—without losing themselves in worldly distractions.
Everything we do is driven by a higher calling. While our initiatives are independent and not officially affiliated with the university, they are guided by a commitment to eternal values. Our focus is on nurturing spiritual growth, building meaningful connections, and empowering students to live out their faith with conviction.

Our Aim

Our aim is to build a strong Christian foundation among tertiary students by nurturing spiritual growth, encouraging authentic discipleship, and equipping them to live out their faith beyond university life. We are committed to raising a generation of godly leaders who are grounded in Scripture, led by the Spirit, and driven by a clear sense of purpose to impact their campuses, communities, and the world for Christ.

Our Mission

Our mission is to:

  • Proclaim the gospel of Jesus Christ to students and staff on campus.
  • Disciple and mentor young believers to grow in faith and live Christ-centered lives.
  • Encourage active engagement in prayer, worship, Bible study, and Christian fellowship.
  • Serve the university and broader community with compassion, integrity, and love.

Our Vision

We envision a vibrant fellowship of spiritually mature, mission-minded students who are transforming Unitech, Papua New Guinea, and the world with the love and truth of Jesus Christ. Our desire is to see a revival begin in the hearts of students—young men and women who carry Christ into every sphere of influence and lead lives that reflect His light and purpose.
