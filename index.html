Welcome to University of Technology TSCF Students in Campus


Tertiary Students Christian Fellowship (TSCF) at the University of Technology (Unitech) is a vibrant, student-led Christian community dedicated to helping students grow in faith, develop godly character, and impact campus and society for Christ. As part of a nationwide movement, we aim to be a light on campus—building unity among believers and reaching out with the love of Jesus Christ.

Whether you're a first-year student looking for a spiritual home or a returning student eager to grow in your walk with God, TSCF is here for you. We invite you to explore, connect, and be part of a family that seeks to live out the gospel in word and deed.

Who We Are

Tertiary Students Christian Fellowship (TSCF) Unitech is a dynamic, Christ-centered community of students passionate about growing in faith and making a difference. We exist to support, equip, and empower students to live for Jesus both on campus and in everyday life. Whether you're curious about Christianity or looking to deepen your walk with God, TSCF is a place where you’ll find genuine fellowship, encouragement, and purpose. Together, we seek to know Christ and make Him known — in our university, in our relationships, and in the world.

What We Do

At TSCF Unitech, we’re committed to helping students grow in their relationship with God, build authentic community, and actively live out their faith on campus. Our weekly programs offer a welcoming space for everyone — whether you're exploring Christianity for the first time or have been following Jesus for years. Through Bible studies, prayer gatherings, social events, and campus outreach, we journey together to know Christ more deeply and make Him known in our university community.

People Behind the Ministry

Behind TSCF Unitech is a dedicated team of student leaders and ministry partners who serve with passion, purpose, and prayer. These individuals play a vital role in guiding the vision of the ministry, supporting fellow students, and creating spaces where faith can flourish. Committed to living out the Gospel with integrity, they work together to help others grow in their relationship with God and make a meaningful impact on campus and beyond.

Come Find Us

We gather every Saturday evening at 7:00 PM at the Christian Centre (CC) on the University of Technology campus. Whether you’re exploring faith, looking for community, or ready to get involved, you’re always welcome here. Come just as you are, meet new friends, and discover what God is doing through TSCF Unitech. We’d love to see you there!
