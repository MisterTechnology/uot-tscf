Meet Our Executives

The Tertiary Students Christian Fellowship (TSCF) is led by a passionate and dedicated team of student leaders committed to serving God and their fellow students. Our executive team oversees the vision, planning, and day-to-day running of the fellowship, ensuring that every student is supported, equipped, and encouraged in their spiritual journey.
